About the App

ShoppinLyst helps you manage your shopping habits by creating lists and locating nearby grocery stores. Shop smarter, stay organized, and save time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new list?
Tap the “+” icon, enter a name, and start adding items.

Can I use the app offline?
Yes, list features work offline, but store locations require internet access.

How do I find grocery stores near me?
Enable location access in settings, and the app will show nearby stores.
